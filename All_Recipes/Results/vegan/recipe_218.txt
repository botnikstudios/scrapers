Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C). Prepare a 12x8-inch baking dish with cooking spray.
Pour entire contents of can of black beans into a blender bowl; add water and blend until very smooth.
Stir blended black beans and brownie mix together in a bowl until the mix is completely moistened; pour into prepared baking dish.
Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, about 40 minutes.
